Autino

A Responsive Ecosystem that collects data, gives actionable feedback and provides critical sensory intervention for Autistic children

Concept

Autism is characterized by impairments in communication, behavior and social interaction. Currently, there is no known cure for autism. However, as quoted by the National Autism Association, “with early intervention and treatment, the diverse symptoms related to autism can be greatly improved and in some cases completely overcome.“

As part of our course work at Cornell University, our team of 5 students took on the grand challenge of addressing issues faced by individuals with autism, in particular for children in their early years of development.

We developed the design concept of the Autino ecosystem, an ambient installation at schools for autistic children and created a prototype for reporting data that would enable caregivers, parents and clinicians to plan and execute effective and evidence-based interventions.
